How To Use

Searching for a car is as easy as: !autotrader search cars You can also search for bikes and vans.
Finding a vehicle that meets your specific tastes is a little more involved but is still very straightforward!
If you're looking to find a new Tesla, you'd type the following: !autotrader search cars --make Tesla --condition New The words prefixed with double hyphens (--) are called flags and there are a bunch of them that you can use to narrow down your search.
You can find a complete list of them here but these are some of the most common:

Flag Definition
--make The make of the vehicle (e.g. Tesla, Ford, Nissan)
--model The model of the vehicle (e.g. Model S, Focus, Skyline)
--variant The variant of the vehicle (e.g. ST, EcoBoost)
--min-year --max-year The minimum or maximum year of manufacture
--min-price --max-price The minimum or maximum price (in ¬£) to search for
--results The amount of results to get from the search

NOTE: If your input contains a space (e.g. Range Rover), put some quotes around it so it's understood as a single value (e.g. "Ranger Rover")
